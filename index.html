💬
Problem

A small business was losing 60% of inbound WhatsApp leads due to slow response times. The sales team was spending 4+ hours/day on repetitive first-touch conversations, leaving high-value tasks unaddressed.

Solution Overview

Manufacturing AI Assistant

A RAG-powered AI assistant embedded into factory floor operations, enabling maintenance engineers to instantly retrieve answers from thousands of pages of SOPs, equipment manuals, and troubleshooting documents.

🏭
Problem

Engineers spent 2–3 hours searching through outdated PDFs and physical manuals during equipment downtime. Knowledge silos caused delays and quality escapes. Senior experts were being pulled away from critical tasks to answer basic questions.

Solution Overview

Built a RAG pipeline ingesting 500+ documents into a vector database (Pinecone). Deployed a Claude-powered assistant with semantic retrieval, citation tracking, and confidence scoring — accessible via web interface and Slack bot for factory teams.

Multi-Agent Orchestration

AI Multi-Agent Hub

A modular multi-agent system where specialized AI agents — Researcher, Writer, Editor, Publisher — collaborate autonomously to produce high-quality content at scale with minimal human oversight.

🕸️
Problem

Content teams were bottlenecked by the research-to-publish pipeline. Each piece required coordination across 4–5 people and took 5–7 days. The client needed 10x output with the same team size.

Solution Overview

Architected a LangGraph-based multi-agent workflow with 4 specialized agents: a Researcher (web search + synthesis), Writer (long-form generation), Editor (quality scoring + revisions), and Publisher (CMS integration). A supervisor agent routes tasks and manages state.
